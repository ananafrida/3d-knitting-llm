      Little Cottage is a pattern for very quick home decorative project. You may knit it both for yourself and as a gift.
      Yarn needed: Drops Karisma no. 72 (about 5 - 7 metres), Drops Karisma no. 60 (about 5 metres) or two contrast colours of any DK yarn, a little bit of black thread for window embroidery.
      Materials needed: double pointed needles size 2.5; durning needle, stitch marker, twine, little carabiner, decorative pendant.
