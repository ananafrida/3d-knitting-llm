      …let me shout out a big THANK YOU for your love, your ongoing support, your understanding, your patience….for being by my side through this difficult year …. hope you enjoy this hat pattern!

      For pattern support please go to the help thread here.

      Jelka is a casual hat, worked in the round from the folded brim to the top. The cute pattern on this hat is knitted with only one color on any given round. Working slip stitches, you get a beautiful colorwork without having to carry two threads in one round, making it the perfect introduction to colorwork. An optional pompom is worked last.

      Yarn
      Worsted weight yarn, approximately
      MC: 148 (161, 174) yds /135 (147, 159) m
      CC: 14 (18, 22) yds /13 (16, 20) m
      Note: Yardage doesn’t include optional pompom.

      Rosa Pomar Brusca (136 yds (125 m)/50g); shown in color B (MC) and 6B (CC)

      Needles
      US 5 (3.75 mm) circular needle, 16” (40 cm) long for brim.
      US 6 (4 mm) circular needle, 16” (40 cm) long and DPNs for body.
      needle sizes are suggestions only, please make a gauge swatch to determine the needle size needed to get gauge

      Gauge
      20 sts x 30 rows = 4” (10 cm) square in Stockinette stitch on larger needle after blocking.

      Sizes
      Circumference (unstretched):
      18 (19.5, 21)“ / 46 (49.5, 53.5) cm
      to fit
      20/21.5 (21.5/23, 23/24.5)”
      51/54.5 (54.5/58.5, 58.5/62) cm

      Notions
      Smooth waste yarn and crochet hook for provisional cast on, 8 stitch markers, tapestry needle, pompom maker (optional), blocking tools.
