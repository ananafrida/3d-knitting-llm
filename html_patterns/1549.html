      The Pumpkin Patch is an adorable pattern that will help you knit the most beautiful pumpkins for the Autumn season!

      The instructions are available in the round and flat so you can make it as you prefer.
      And there’s a video tutorial to help you shape and finish your pumpkin!!

      PATTERN INFORMATION:

      • Available Sizes: approx. diameter of 8, 11, 14 cm / 3,25”, 4,25”, 5,5” or made to measure
      • Construction: Knitted in the round or knitted flat
      • Experience level: Advanced Beginner.
      • Yarn: I used worsted weight yarn but you can use any yarn of your choice
      • Needles: I used 4mm (US 6) needles, but you can use any needles size of your choice
      • Languages: English, Portuguese and Spanish
