      This pattern is available for free.

      This is a very popular, easy-to-knit little baby cardigan jacket.

      Great for a new baby - boy or girl.

      Knitted top-down with a garter stitch yoke

      A simple and elegant design

      Add cute buttons for a contemporary look

      Sizes:
      Newborn, 3 months, 6 - 9 months, 9 - 12 months (preemie sizes also available)

      Requirements:
      DK (8ply) yarn
      4mm needles
      3 or 6 buttons

      To get this free pattern please click on the https link just below
      ↓

      The original pattern was for a 3 button version.
      And - due to popular demand I have now added a fully-buttoned version too!

      Matching hat, and booties are also available, if you wish to make a little set..

      A lovely gender-neutral design.

      For more information, see: http://mariannaslazydaisydays.blogspot.co.uk/2015/01...
