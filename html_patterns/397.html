      Step out in style with this high fashion faux fur coat! With a cropped silhouette and bracelet length sleeves, you’ll be runway ready in this shaggy chic number that’s also suitable for holiday parties, date nights, or just running errands around town.

      Shop the full kit here.

      SIZES
      XS (S, M, L, XL) (2XL, 3XL, 4XL, 5XL)
      Suggested positive ease: 10-14”/25.5-35.5cm
      Pictured in a size XS/S on model with 34”/86.5cm bust

      FINISHED DIMENSIONS
      Body circumference: 40 (44.75, 48, 52.75, 56) (60.75, 64, 68.75, 72)”/101.5 (113.5, 122, 134, 142) (154.5, 162.5, 174.5, 183)cm
      Length: 18.5 (19, 19.5, 20, 20.5) (21, 21.5, 22, 22.5)”/47 (48.5, 49.5, 51, 52) (53.5, 54.5, 56, 57)cm

      DIFFICULTY LEVEL
