      This pattern is available as a free Ravelry download

      This is a pattern for a small hanging ornament. The instructions are for making a bear but it can also be used to make a rabbit by using the ears from my free ‘Teeny Tiny knitted toys’ pattern here: https://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/teeny-tiny-knitt...

      The pattern uses only small scraps of yarn and can be quickly knitted up, the assembly is a little fiddly and you can save time by making the dress part a few rows longer and not bothering with making the legs.

      Hope you have fun with it, J x

      Updated to a new pdf file, December 15th 2021
