      Set of jacket knitted from side to side, socks and bonnet for baby and children in DROPS Fabel

      ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
      Size: 1/3 - 6/9 - 12/18 months (2 - 3/4) years
      Size in cm: 50/56-62/68-74/80 (86/92-98/104)

      Bust: 46-52-60 (64-70) cm / 18-20-23 (25-27)
      Full length: 27-29-33 (37-40) cm / 10-11-13 (14-15)

      Materials: DROPS FABEL from Garnstudio
      Color no 161, pink dream:
      WHOLE OUTFIT: 200-200-250 (300-350) g
      JACKET ONLY: 150-150-200 (200-250) g

      DROPS POINTED and DOUBLE POINTED Needles size 2.5 mm / US 1 or 2 or size needed to get correct gauge.

      DROPS WOODEN BUTTONS no 513: 5 pcs

      PATTERN LINKS:

      French version
      Norwegian version

      Jacket knitted sideways, socks, and hat

      See socks here and b16-6 Little Jamboree Hat
