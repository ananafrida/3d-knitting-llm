      Just try it....

      by Susan Ashcroft
      Stitchnerd Designs
      Knitting
      Neck / Torso → Cowl
      December 2013
      Malabrigo Yarn Rios
      Gift of Grace Handspun 2 ply
      Worsted (9 wpi) ?
      US 8 - 5.0 mm
      200 - 400 yards (183 - 366 m)
      sizing table - 3 single sizes (2 of which can be doubled) - yarn weights from sock to worsted, giving you lots of options.

      Took a cowl over for a friend.

      Asked her silver-haired, bike-riding, ultra marathon-running (100k) husband if hed wear one - he just spluttered.

      So I asked him to try it on -
      within less than a minute HE was explaining to me in great technical detail why this was the perfect item for all of his outdoor pursuits.

      Honestly, much more practical for active guys than a scarf - if only you can get them to try.

      For snow clearing: it can be pulled up over the face
      For wood-cutting: no danger of it getting caught in the chainsaw

      Some of the guys like a closer-fit, so I added a tighter guy size.

      Sizes ladies might like are included too.

      Many thanks to the testers (and their male models ):
