      Gift Leaves

      by Marijke van der Horst
      Merula Designs
      Knitting
      Applique / Embellishment
      June 2022
      Lana Grossa Meilenweit
      Regia 4-fädig Color
      The Natural Dye Studio Dazzle Sock
      Lang Yarns Super Soxx Cashmere Color 4 ply
      Fingering (14 wpi) ?
      28 stitches and 64 rows = 4 inches
      in Garter stitch
      US 1 - 2.25 mm
      4 - 9 yards (4 - 8 m)
      S, M and L
      Dutch English
      • one-piece
      • worked-flat
      • written-pattern
      • search patterns with these attributes
      This pattern is available as a free Ravelry download

      Made from minute quantities of leftover (sock) yarn, these leaves make lovely little gift tags. Or they can become gifts in their own right – ideas and pictures can be found in the pattern. Just the kind of project for a few stolen moments: portable, easy once you’ve made a couple, and finished in no time. The instructions are for three different sizes.

      Finished measurements
      Approx. 3 x 4 cm (1.2 x 1.6”); 3.5 x 5.5 cm (1.4 x 2.2”); 4.5 x 6.5 cm (1.8 x 2.6”), width x height for sizes S, M and L respectively, excluding the stalk.

      More information and inspiration can be found in this blog post.

      A blog post about how to make an earrings-and-pendant set using this pattern (picture bottom left) can be found here.
