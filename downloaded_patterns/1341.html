      Whimsical Witch Hat

      by Natasha Hanson
      Wee Folk Valley
      Knitting
      Softies → Other
      September 2023
      Fleece & Harmony Point Prim Sock
      Fingering (14 wpi) ?
      8 stitches and 8 rows = 1 inch
      in 8 stitches and 8 rows = 1" in stockinette
      US 2 - 2.75 mm
      6 - 7 yards (5 - 6 m)
      Diameter & Height: 3/ 7.5cm, or desired size.

      This pattern is available as a free Ravelry download

      These hats are fun and quick to make! They are knit entirely in the round on dpns, starting at the brims outward edge, with pretty decreases to the center. A Latvian Braid makes an easy and impressive looking sash (how to video included).

      Praise for Decorative Witch Hat

      Such a fun, little pattern! Made in a minute. And Ive learned a new technique: the Latvian Braid. (Adente)

      This little hat can be made in a hour. For the Latvian braid there is good link of how to do it. So no reason to give it a try. (Saphiera)

      Adorable little witch hat! So easy and quick. I liked the link to the Latvian Braid video-good refresher for the technique. Now to do one more (thrsedge)

      Cute little hat and a well written, easy to follow pattern! (MinniStitcher)

      Knitting the Decorative Witch Hat was so easy, particularly since I already was familiar with knitting in the round. I had never done Latvian Braid stitch before, but the instructions made is really clear, and the results looked great. (fjorlief)
