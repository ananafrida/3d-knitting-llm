You will begin at the tail end and work in stockinette.\n\nUsing MC and your preferred method, CO 17 sts.\n\nRow 1 [RS]: K\nRow 2 [WS]: P\nRow 3: K1, k2tog, k to last 3 st, k2tog, k1. 2 sts decreased.\nRow 4: Purl.\nRows 5-10: Repeat Rows 3-4 three more times. 9 sts.\nRow 11: K1, RLI, k to last 2 sts, LLI, k1. 2 sts increased.\nRow 12: Purl.\nRows 13-16: Repeat Rows 11-12 two more times. 15 sts.\nRow 17: Knit.\nRow 18: P1, RLI, p to last 2 sts, LLI, p1. 17 sts.\nRows 19-24: Work 6 rows even in stockinette, starting with a RS/knit row.\n\nRow 25: K1, k2tog, k to last 3 st, k2tog, k1. 2 sts decreased.\nRow 26: P1, p2tog, p to last 3 st, p2tog, p1. 2 sts decreased.\nRows 27-30: Repeat Rows 25-26 2 more times. 5 sts.\n\nYou are now at the nose end and will continue, working in nose-to-tail direction to make Cheddar's second half.\n\nRow 31: Knit.\nRow 32: P1, RLI, p to last 2 sts, LLI, p1. 2 sts increased.\nRow 33: K1, RLI, k to last 2 sts, LLI, k1. 2 sts increased.\nRows 34-37: Repeat Rows 32-33 two more times. 17 sts.\n\nRows 38-43: Work 6 rows even in stockinette, starting with a WS/purl row.\nRow 44: P1, p2tog, p to last 3 sts, p2tog, p1. 15 sts.\nRow 45-46: Work 2 rows even in stockinette\nRow 47: K1, k2tog, k to last 3 sts, k2tog, k1. 2 sts decreased.\nRow 48: Purl.\nRows 49-52: Repeat Rows 47-48 two more times. 9 sts.\n\nRow 53: K1, RLI, k to last 2 sts, LLI, k1. 2 sts increased.\nRow 54: Purl.\nRows 55-60: Repeat Rows 53-54 3 more times. 17 sts.\n\nBind off.
